About Me

I am a professor at the Carlos III University of Madrid in Spain, in the SP group, joining IMDEA Software this December 2025. During 2022-2024, I was a postdoc at the Zuse Institute Berlin (ZIB) and lead of the Continuous Optimization branch in the IOL group, where I worked with Sebastian Pokutta among others. Prior to this, I was a PhD student at the Department of Computer Science at the University of Oxford supervised by Prof. Varun Kanade and Prof. Patrick Rebeschini. I obtained an MSc in Mathematics and Foundations of Computer Science at the University of Oxford and a double Bachelor in Mathematics and in Computer Science at the Complutense University of Madrid.

I am looking for PhD students and postdocs to work in Madrid, Spain. Spread the word!

  • Highly international department at UC3M’s SP group and IMDEA Software, and working language is English.
  • For PhDs: strong math skills are required: formalize problems, writing rigorous proofs, etc.
  • For postdocs: I seek prior experience in optimization / online learning / machine learning theory with publications in top venues.
  • Send an email with the subject “PhD Application” or “Postdoc Application”. Write why you are interested in working with me and why you are a good fit. Include your detailed CV, and academic transcripts. Contacts for asking for letters of recommendation are also beneficial. If you apply for a postdoc, further send a research statement outlining your past work and future research plans, and google scholar or equivalent.
